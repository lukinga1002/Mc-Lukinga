Who is MC Lukinga?

MC Lukinga, known as Mr Wonderful, is Dar es Salaam's premier event host and Master of Ceremonies. He specializes in wedding MC services, corporate event hosting, and private party emceeing — known for his unique combination of class, entertainment, and fun.

What events does MC Lukinga host?

MC Lukinga hosts weddings, corporate events (conferences, award ceremonies, product launches, galas), and private parties (birthdays, anniversaries, milestone celebrations). He is available across Tanzania and internationally — including South Africa, Dubai, and Botswana.
